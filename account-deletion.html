Account Deletion

You can request account deletion from within the CrewOS app. Deletion is permanent and removes:

  • Profile & authentication record
  • Jobs you own (collaborators lose access)
  • Invoices & generated PDFs
  • Stored attachments (images, PDFs) owned by you

Some minimal log or aggregate data may remain for security, anti-fraud, or legal compliance.

In-App Deletion

  1. Open the app and sign in
  2. Navigate to Profile > Account Settings
  3. Select Delete Account and confirm

Email Request (If You Lack Access)

Email support@crewos.app from your registered address with subject: Account Deletion Request.

Processing Time

We aim to process verified requests within 7 days.
